MONEY HEIST

Money Heist (Spanish: La casa de papel, lit. 'The House of Paper') is a Spanish heist crime drama series created by Álex Pina.
The series follows two elaborate multi-day heists orchestrated by the enigmatic Professor (Álvaro Morte),
with the narrative often presented from the perspective of one of the robbers, Tokyo (Úrsula Corberó).

Key members of the heist team include the mastermind The Professor (Sergio Marquina),
the narrator Tokyo (Silene Oliveira), Berlin (Andrés de Fonollosa), Nairobi (Ágata Jiménez),
Rio (Aníbal Cortés), and Lisbon (Raquel Murillo), a police inspector who eventually joins the gang.
