GAME OF THRONES

Game of Thrones is an acclaimed, Emmy Award-winning HBO epic fantasy drama series based on George R.R. Martin's novel series A Song of Ice and Fire.
It chronicles the power struggles among noble families for control of the Iron Throne of the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros.

The show ran for eight seasons and 73 episodes, from April 17, 2011, to May 19, 2019. It was widely praised for its acting,
complex characters, and high production values, though its final season received some criticism for its creative decisions.
