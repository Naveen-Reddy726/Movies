ALICE IN BORDERLAND

Alice in Borderland is a Japanese survival suspense franchise that originated as a manga and has
been adapted into a popular live-action Netflix series. It follows characters who are trapped in a desolate,
parallel version of Tokyo and forced to compete in dangerous, life-or-death games to survive.



Season 1 (2020): Focuses on the initial adjustment to the Borderland, the rules of the games, and the discovery of
"the Beach," a player stronghold.
Season 2 (2022): Players face off against the "face card" citizens of the Borderland to collect the remaining cards and find
a way back to the real world.
Season 3 (2025): An original story beyond the manga, where Arisu and Usagi are forced back into the Borderland to face the final
"Joker" card and ultimately decide their future
