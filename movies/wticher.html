THE WITCHER

"The Witcher" is a popular dark fantasy franchise originating from a series of books by Polish author Andrzej Sapkowski,
which has since expanded into highly successful video games and a Netflix live-action series

The franchise centers on Geralt of Rivia, a magically enhanced monster hunter (known as a "witcher")
who struggles to find his place in a world where people often prove more wicked than beasts.
