THE BOYZ

"The Boys" is a popular and critically acclaimed superhero media franchise, best known for its live-action
television series on Prime Video, which deconstructs the superhero genre with a dark, satirical,
and R-rated take. The franchise is based on the comic book series of the same name by Garth Ennis and Darick Robertson.

The main story follows a group of vigilantes, informally known as "The Boys," led by Billy Butcher, as they
fight against corrupt and often arrogant superheroes (or "Supes") who abuse their powers and celebrity status.
Most of the Supes work for a powerful and marketing-obsessed corporation called Vought International.
The primary adversaries for The Boys are Vought's premier superhero team, "The Seven,
" led by the narcissistic and unstable Homelander.
