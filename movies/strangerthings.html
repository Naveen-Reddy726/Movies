STRANGER THINGS

Stranger Things currently has five seasons, with a total of 42 episodes. The final season (Season 5)
is currently being released in three segments throughout November and December 2025.

Season 1 (2016)
Season 1 consists of eight episodes, all released on July 15, 2016.
"Chapter One: The Vanishing of Will Byers"
"Chapter Two: The Weirdo on Maple Street"
"Chapter Three: Holly, Jolly"
"Chapter Four: The Body"
"Chapter Five: The Flea and the Acrobat"
"Chapter Six: The Monster"
"Chapter Seven: The Bathtub"
"Chapter Eight: The Upside Down"
Season 2 (2017)
Season 2 consists of nine episodes, all released on October 27, 2017.
Season 3 (2019)
Season 3, released on July 4, 2019, has eight episodes, including titles like "Suzie, Do You Copy?".
Season 4 (2022)
Season 4 consists of nine episodes. Episodes include "The Hellfire Club".
Season 5 (2025)
Season 5 is being released in three segments.
