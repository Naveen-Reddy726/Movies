Salaar: Part 1 -Ceasefire is a 2023 Telugu-language Indian action-drama directed by Prashanth Neel,
starring Prabhas as the titular Deva and Prithviraj Sukumaran as his friend Vardha, exploring their
deep bond amidst the violent power struggles of the fictional kingdom of Khansaar, featuring massive action,
intense world-building, and ending with a cliffhanger setting up the sequel, Salaar: Part 2.
